Welcome to Sri Venkateswara Sawmill, a leading fully integrated timber products company since 2007. With expertise in procurement, stocking, sawmill manufacturing operations, and furniture manufacturing, we are your trusted partner for premium timber solutions.

Our success hinges on our adept procurement strategies, ensuring the acquisition of diverse wood classes from both international and local sources. As renowned stockists of timber from various regions of India, we boast a comprehensive inventory featuring an extensive array of species, including Teak wood, Rosewood, Mango wood, Acacia wood, Subabul wood, Neem wood, Jamun wood, and Eucalyptus wood. At Sri Venkateswara Sawmill, quality is paramount.

We meticulously grade our wood based on moisture content and soil type, implementing rigorous quality control measures throughout the seasoning process. This meticulous attention to detail not only eliminates the risk of fungal and bacterial growth but also enhances the distinct grain patterns characteristic of each timber species.
